Ancient History

Formation of Solara

2.2 Billion Years Ago

Solara, Aeloria's red dwarf, emerged from a dense cosmic cloud. Gravity caused this cloud to condense into a rotating protostellar disk. Over time, nuclear fusion reactions ignited at its core, leading to the birth of Solara. Initially, it emitted brilliant light, but it eventually evolved into an ultra rare stable and calm red dwarf, much brighter then Sol, providing Aeloria with consistent illumination.
